My Personal Logo

This versatile design has proven itself adapatble and eyecatching over the years. Fresh out of design school I sat in a coffeeshop for 6 hours in downtown Detroit to hammer this out. In using it to introduce this website, I proved a philosopical idea of the Bauhaus and Paul Rand - Good design has staying power.

In the summer of 2022 I was tapped to orchestrate the summer digatal ad campaign for the midwest which they wanted centered around Detroit. In terms of peope seeing my work, this was the most successful project I've ever put together with millions of people seeing these ads.
