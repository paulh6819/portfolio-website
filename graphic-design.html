My Personal Logo

This versatile design has proven itself adapatble and eyecatching over the years. Fresh out of design school I sat in a coffeeshop for 6 hours in downtown Detroit to hammer this out. The result was a functional, beatiful, unique design that coninues to produce even years later. In using it to introduce this website, I proved a philosopical idea of the Bauhaus and Paul Rand - Good design has staying power.
